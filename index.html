Reachly — Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 22, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Reachly ("we", "us", or "our") collects and uses your information.

1. Introduction

By using Reachly you accept the practices described in this Privacy Policy. Reachly is a platform to discover influencers and businesses and to connect for promotional collaborations.

2. Information We Collect

  • Account & Authentication: mobile number used for OTP-based authentication.
  • Profile Data: display name, photo, role (user/influencer/business), category, bio, profile media.
  • Preferences & Actions: saved/blocked lists, search preferences, advertising selections.
  • Device & Usage: device model, OS, IP address, crash logs and analytics to improve service.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use information to authenticate accounts, display relevant profiles and nearby results, enable save/block features, provide advertising/promotion tools, maintain security, and improve the app.

4. Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data. We may share information with:

  • Other users when you publish a public influencer or business profile.
  • Service providers who help operate the app (SMS/OTP providers, hosting, analytics).
  • Authorities if required by law or to prevent fraud or abuse.

5. Current Status — No Verification & No Charges

No Verification: Reachly currently does not provide official platform verification. Profiles are not vouchsafed by Reachly unless explicitly stated in the app in future updates.

No Charges: Reachly is currently free for basic use. If we introduce paid features, verification services, or subscriptions in the future, we'll notify users in advance and publish updated terms and pricing.

6. Security

We use reasonable technical and administrative safeguards to protect your information. However, no system is completely secure. Do not share OTPs, passwords, or financial details with others.

7. Your Rights

You can update or delete your profile and request deletion of your account data. To request account deletion or for data access, use the in-app settings or contact us (see below). We will respond in accordance with applicable law.

8. Reporting Abuse & Scams

Because Reachly does not currently verify profiles, there may be risk of scammers or impersonators. Verify users independently before any financial or business transaction. Report abuse via the in-app reporting tool — include screenshots and details to help our review.

9. Children

Reachly is not intended for children under 13. If we learn an account belongs to a child under 13, we will take appropriate steps to remove the data.
